What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, Visa, Mastercard, and PayPal. All payments must be completed before an order is processed.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery within Nairobi takes 1–2 business days. Deliveries outside Nairobi may take 2–4 business days depending on location.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After this window, cancellation may not be possible if the order has already been processed.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS. You can use this tracking number on our website to check delivery status.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only deliver within Kenya.

What should I do if I receive a damaged item?

If you receive a damaged item, contact customer support within 48 hours with photos of the product and packaging.
