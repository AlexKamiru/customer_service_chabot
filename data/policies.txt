Refund Policy

Customers may request a refund within 30 days of receiving their order. Refunds are processed once the returned item is inspected and approved.

Items must be returned in their original packaging and in unused condition. Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

Return Policy

To initiate a return, customers must contact customer support and obtain a return authorization number. Returns without authorization will not be processed.

Privacy Policy

We collect customer information solely for order processing and service improvement. We do not sell or share personal information with third parties without consent.

Exchange Policy

Exchanges are allowed within 14 days of delivery for unused items. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item was defective.
